RehaLab forum is a place, where you can post your questions, comment, and discuss problems, regarding your rehabilitation programm.

RehaLab is a research laboratory that develops robotic rehabilitation systems for various applications: rehabilitation robots for upper limb and lower limb; rehabilitation robots for home and clinical use; rehabilitation systems for clinical use; rehabilitation equipment for home; training rehabilitation for upper and lower limb; gait analysis; Electrostimulation device for stroke rehabilitation, etc.
