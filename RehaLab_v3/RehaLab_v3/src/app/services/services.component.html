RehaLab Services

RehaLab offers the following services: Design, development and research of: Systems for rehabilitation and support of human movements; Interfaces for communication and control of active orthoses and exoskeletons for upper and lower limbs; Biomechanical analysis of human movements; Development of rehabilitation procedures based on virtual reality scenes; Research and applied activities in the field of mechatronics and rehabilitation robotics.

  • Systems for rehabilitation
  • Biomechanical analysis
  • Gait analysis
  • Motion capture systems
  • Virtual reality scenes
